# DRIFT Launch Inputs

This is what still needs your decision or real business information before DRIFT can become a true live store.

## Product and Brand

- Final confirmation on product names
- Final confirmation on prices
- Exact color names
- Fabric composition from your supplier
- Size/spec details for pillowcases, bonnets, scrunchies, and sets
- Real product photos or samples for photography
- Final logo files as transparent PNG/SVG if available

## Store Setup

- Preferred platform: Shopify, Webflow, Squarespace, Vercel, Netlify, or another host
- Payment provider
- Shipping rates and regions
- Return/exchange policy
- Business email address
- Domain name

## Marketing

- Email provider: Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Shopify Email, ConvertKit, or another option
- Supabase Project URL and public anon key when you want live email capture connected
- Launch date or target month
- Instagram/TikTok handles
- Any reviews, testimonials, creator quotes, or press language
